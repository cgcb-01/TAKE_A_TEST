Q: What is the jelly-like substance inside the cell?
A) Nucleoplasm
B) Cytoplasm
C) Lysosome
D) Mitochondria
Answer: B
Solution: Cytoplasm holds organelles and is the site of most cellular activities.

Q: Which organelle is responsible for packaging proteins?
A) Ribosomes
B) Golgi apparatus
C) Endoplasmic reticulum
D) Lysosomes
Answer: B
Solution: Golgi apparatus modifies and packages proteins for secretion or use.
