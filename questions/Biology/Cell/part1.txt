Q: What is the function of mitochondria?
A) Photosynthesis
B) Protein synthesis
C) Energy production
D) Waste removal
Answer: C
Solution: Mitochondria are known as the powerhouse of the cell and produce ATP.

Q: Which of the following is only found in plant cells?
A) Nucleus
B) Ribosome
C) Cell wall
D) Mitochondria
Answer: C
Solution: Plant cells have a rigid cell wall that animal cells do not possess.
