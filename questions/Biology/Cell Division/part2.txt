Q: In which phase does cytokinesis typically occur?
A) Prophase
B) Metaphase
C) Telophase
D) Interphase
Answer: C
Solution: Cytokinesis usually begins during or just after telophase to complete cell division.

Q: How many daughter cells are formed after mitosis?
A) 1
B) 2
C) 3
D) 4
Answer: B
Solution: Mitosis produces two genetically identical daughter cells.
