Q: What is the phase in which chromosomes line up at the cell equator?
A) Prophase
B) Metaphase
C) Anaphase
D) Telophase
Answer: B
Solution: In metaphase, chromosomes align at the metaphase plate or equator of the cell.

Q: What process ensures genetic diversity during meiosis?
A) Binary fission
B) Cytokinesis
C) Crossing over
D) Replication
Answer: C
Solution: Crossing over occurs during prophase I of meiosis and promotes genetic variation.
