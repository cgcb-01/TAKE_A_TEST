Q: What is the sum of the first 5 terms of the GP: 2, 4, 8, ...?
A) 62
B) 60
C) 64
D) 66
Answer: C
Solution: S5 = a(r^5 - 1)/(r - 1) = 2(32 - 1)/(2 - 1) = 62

Q: If the 3rd term of a GP is 8 and the 5th term is 32, find the common ratio.
A) 2
B) 3
C) 4
D) 5
Answer: A
Solution: Let 3rd term be ar^2 = 8, 5th = ar^4 = 32 → divide → r^2 = 4 → r = 2
